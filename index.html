About us

We'll take care of your Health

Galgotias Hospital is a multi-specialty hospital located in Greater Noida, Uttar Pradesh, India. The hospital provides a wide range of medical services, including cardiology, neurology, orthopedics, gastroenterology, and oncology, among others.

The hospital has a team of highly qualified and experienced doctors, nurses, and other medical staff. In addition, the hospital also offers various facilities such as 24-hour emergency services, pharmacy, laboratory, radiology, and blood bank, among others.
